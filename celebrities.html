Suri—TomKat’s spoiled sweetie— is one of the most talked about tots in Hollywood.

Born on April 18, 2006 in Santa Monica, Calif., the born star entered the world in a storm of controversy. Initially, there was speculation that Suri was not Tom Cruise‘s daughter, but actually a product of impregnating Katie Holmes with the preserved sperm of the founder of Scientology, L. Ron Hubbard. These rumors have since subsided.

The toddler has many loves, including the Spice Girls, tap dancing, getting manicures, and, of course, her bottle! Will the celebrity child follow her parent’s footsteps and pursue a career in acting? “She’s already a wonderful artist. If she wants to [act] great, if she doesn’t, OK,” says mom Holmes.
