Meet Jeff Zients, The New White House Chief of Staff

Jeff Zients, aka Jeffrey Dunston Zients (born November 12, 1966), is a 56-year-old American businessman and politician. In 2001, Fortune Magazine ranked him 25 (one rank behind Elon Musk) on the list of 40 wealthiest self-made Americans.

His political career started as Deputy Director of the Office of Management and Budget for Management during the Barack Obama government until 2013. After serving in various positions till 2017, he returned in January 2021, serving as White House Coronavirus Response Coordinator.

In January 2023, Zients started appearing in headlines after it got announced that he would be the next Chief of Staff, replacing Ron Klain. According to CNN, the exact of Zients’s appointment as COS is not set, but it is expected to happen in the first week of February.

Jeff Zients’s Net Worth & Political Career

For years Zients worked in different organizations in different roles, but his career took a fantastic leap after he joined the Advisory Board Company and Corporate Executive Board. He helped both companies go public and earned millions of dollars. A Fortune Magazine article revealed Jeffrey’s net worth to be $149 million in 2001.

Four years later, he, Fred Malek, and Colin Powell allied to compete in the bidding for the Baseball team Washington Nationals but lost to the Lerner family. Jeff may not use Facebook, but he did earn a lot of money by serving as the auditor for years. Reports revealed that he received a compensation of $100k in cash and $300k in stocks.

In 2009, starting a political career with the Democratic party, Zients assumed the position of Deputy Director of the Office of Management and Budget for Management after being chosen by the Obama government.

Meanwhile, he handled the position of Chief Performance Officer. In 2012 he was promoted to the seat of Director of the Office of Management and Budget, where he served only for a year and a half until April 2013.

In 2017, the Jewish millionaire was serving as a Director of the National Economic Council, but with the incoming of Donald Trump on January 20, he got succeeded by Gary Cohn. With Joe Biden coming into power in 2021, Dunston first assumed the position of Counselor to the President and became the White House Coronavirus Response Coordinator.

Who are Jeff Zients’s Parents?

Jeffrey Dunston Zients, aka Jeff Zient (age 56), is from Washington, D.C., U.S., where he was born on November 12, 1966, to his mother, Debbie, and father, Alan Zients. According to sources, Jeff’s Jewish parents raised him in Kensington, Maryland.

He attended a boys-only boarding school named St. Albans School, where he graduated in 1984 and joined Duke University. At DU, Zients studied political science and graduated summa cum laude in 1988.

After college, he entered the corporate world after landing a job as a management consultant at Mercer Management Consulting (currently known as Oliver Wyman). After some time, he changed his job and joined Bain & Company, where he learned a lot about work culture and teamwork.
