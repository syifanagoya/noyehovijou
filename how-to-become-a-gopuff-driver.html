Is Gopuff making money?

Gopuff makes money by marking up products, through delivery fees, subscriptions, as well as advertising on its platform. Founded in 2013, Gopuff has become one of the biggest delivery startups in the United States. Its business is currently valued at $8.9 billion.

Where do Gopuff drivers get their stuff from?

The company generally finds and purchases cheap warehouses to store its products. The warehouses don’t have to look fancy; there are no customers browsing the shelves. The company also hires delivery drivers in the same vein as UberEats, making the company a player in the sharing economy.

Does Gopuff use a warehouse?

Personally owned warehouses: Rather than acting as a service that picks up items from partner businesses, goPuff owns local facilities to house available inventory in the cities it services. Orders simply go directly from the warehouse to the customer, cutting down on the logistics involved.
