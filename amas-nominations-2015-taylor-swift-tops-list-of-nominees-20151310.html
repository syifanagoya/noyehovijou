AMAs Nominations 2015: Taylor Swift Tops List of Nominees

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Roll out the red carpet! The nominations for the 2015 American Music Awards were announced on Tuesday, Oct. 13 — and each category has some stiff competition!

Taylor Swift will face nine other stars for the Artist of the Year category, including Nicki Minaj, Sam Smith, One Direction, and Ariana Grande. If the "Shake It Off" singer takes home all six of her nominations this year, she will become the most decorated female artist in AMAs history, beating the late Whitney Houston's 21 wins. 

First-time nominees, meanwhile, include Ed Sheeran, The Weeknd, and breakout country star Sam Hunt.
