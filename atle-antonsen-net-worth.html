FAQs about Atle Antonsen

Who is Atle Antonsen?

He is a well-known Norwegian comedian, actor, and writer, recognized for his participation in various television and radio shows, as well as feature films.

When and where was Atle Antonsen born?

He was born on August 11, 1969, in Lillehammer, Norway.

What are some notable television shows and movies featuring Atle Antonsen?

He has been part of shows like XLTV, Nissene På Låven, Team Antonsen, and Kongen Befaler. His film roles include Tommy’s Inferno, Kong Curling, and Doktor Proktors Tidsbadekar.

Did he win any awards for his work?

Yes, he has won the Norwegian Comedy Award several times, including Best Stand-up in 2004 and Male Comedian of the Year in 2007, 2008, and 2012.

What are some of Atle Antonsen’s recent projects?

Since 2019, he has hosted Kongen Befaler, the Norwegian version of Taskmaster. He stepped down from the role in 2022 following the controversy but returned in 2023.
