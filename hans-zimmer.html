Biography

Hans Zimmer was born in Frankfurt, Germany on September 12, 1957. Film composer known for his work in more than 150 films, including The Lion King. He’s also known for having a lengthy collaboration with filmmaker Christopher Nolan, working on Inception and The Dark Knight trilogy. He and John Williams became two of modern cinema’s most renowned composers. He was in the New Wave band The Buggles during the latter part of the 1970s and appeared in their music video “Video Killed the Radio Star” from 1979.
