GoFundMe Campaign Launched to Assist a Family Struck By a Recent Tragedy

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Mike Johnson of PWInsider.com alerted the Internet Wrestling Media yesterday that a recent tragedy took place involving a close friend, and although this is not directly wrestling related, it’s for a good cause.

Mike noted he received some pretty terrible news this week, learning that the son of one of his High School friends, Jimmy Ferrie, Sebastian, passed away at the age of only one year and seven months after a battle with Leukemia. You read that correctly – one year and seven months old.

As you can imagine, this is an absolutely terrible tragedy. We all go through our day to day life and deal with the ups and downs and get mad and upset and worried and stressed, but Sebastian’s family, they have been through the worst thing that life can ever possibly throw at someone.

Sebastian’s family pretty much did everything they could, emotionally and especially financially to assist him in his battle, and unfortunately for everyone, there wasn’t a happy ending, only an absolute tragedy that leaves you asking how life could be so unfair.

While unfortunately nothing could be done to help Sebastian, there is something that we can do to help his family as they try to make their way through this darkness and Mike humbly asks that if you can, please assist his friends.

A number of Johnson’s friends from High School have established a GoFundMe.com page to help offset the funeral costs of Sebastian’s services. His family literally spent everything they could to help him in his battle. They have nothing, literally nothing, left and now it’s time for their friends to do what they can as well.

Please feel free to donate at this link: http://www.gofundme.com/nqy3gk?fb_action_ids=10152663201017477&fb_action_types=og.shares.
